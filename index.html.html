About

Data that speaks business,
not just numbers

I'm a data analyst and business educator based in Abuja, Nigeria, with over three years of experience turning messy spreadsheets and raw databases into clear, interactive dashboards that drive real business decisions.

My clients include corporate teams, international NGOs, and SME owners who need more than a pretty chart — they need insight they can act on immediately. I specialize in Power BI dashboard development, SQL-based analysis, and Excel automation.

I also create data education content to help business owners and aspiring analysts understand their numbers without needing a technical background.
